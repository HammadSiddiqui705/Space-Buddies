Stars are giant balls of hot gas that shine bright in space! Our Sun is actually a star - the closest one to Earth. Stars come in different colors: blue stars are very hot, yellow stars like our Sun are medium hot, and red stars are cooler. When stars get very old, some become white dwarfs, and the biggest ones might become black holes!
